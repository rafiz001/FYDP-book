\chapter{Conclusion}

In this chapter we are pointing the consclusion of our project report by providing summary, limitation and future work of our project.

\section{Summary}
Pharmaciano stands as a practical, cost effective, and technologically advanced ERP solution for the pharmacy in Bangladesh. It demonstrates the successful application of modern full stack development, cross platform mobile development, and AI to solve real-world  problems, providing a strong foundation for future growth and enhancement.

\section{Limitation}
Currently there are some limitations too for our project like:
\begin{itemize}
\item No integration with national medicine database. 
\item No e-commerce website to sell medicine directly to customer.
\item No payment gateway implemented.
\item No offline AI model used leading internet dependency.
\item No other mode of AI prediction other than historical sales data quality.
\item No resource independence with the free deployment.
\end{itemize}
\section{Future Work}
We can extend our project by solving the limitations. It includes to add payment gateway that supports local bank cards and MFS services. We can add module to our ERP to add e-commerce website too. We also have to seacrch any nation-wide medicine database to integrate it into our ERP system.
