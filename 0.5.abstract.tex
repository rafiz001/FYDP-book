Pharmaciano is a cross-platform multi-vendor pharmacy ERP system with AI-based stock forecasting and analytics. Existing pharmacy ERP systems have problems like: not a generic solution, lack of product and price awareness, inventory mismanagement, lack of intelligence and prediction. But our approach is targeted, intelligent and affordable. We successfully implemented the ERP system that solves all the problems of existing ERP solution previously mentioned. By using our ERP system it will keep a good impact on public health as well as it will save money and time for pharmacy.
